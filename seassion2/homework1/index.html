Where is Georgia?

Georgia is a transcontinental country in Easter Europe and West Asia. It is a part of the Caucasus region, bounded by the Black sea to the west, Russia to the north and northeast, Turkey to the southwest, Armenia to the south, and Azerbaijan to the southeast.
